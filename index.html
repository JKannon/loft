About theLOFT

theLOFT provides its customers with a fun, friendly environment with lots of fitness options. Whether you like boot camps, circuit training, cardio, TRX or hitting bags or serious boxing training theLOFT has the right class for you. There are multiple classes to choose from each day. We also offer our members access to our personal trainers and use of the facility to guide their own workout.

One program that makes theLOFT unique is our USA Amateur Boxing program at night. If you are interested in competitive boxing, want to learn how to defend yourself or just want to get in shape the Lights Out Boxing program can help. There are classes for youth and adults and focus on conditioning and technique. Lights Out Boxing has a history of developing competitive boxers. Check out the Lights Out website for more information.

Classes & Programming

Lights Out Fitness & Training offers a variety of classes and programming to suit most individual preferences. Please check out our MindBody website for the latest schedule.

Self-Led / Instructor Supervised Programming

When you are in a hurry or just want some solitude. These workouts will allow you to get a great workout in a short amount of time. These are timed, instructor supervised, individual workouts. Workouts change daily.

Lights Out Blitz Contender

30 Minute multi-exercise full-body workout. Two rotations / ten stations that change every 90 seconds. No waiting, rotation starts every 90 seconds.

Lights Out Blitz Champion

45 Minute multi-exercise full-body workout. Three rotations with 10 stations that change every 90 seconds. No waiting, rotation starts every 90 seconds. Progress tracking tools included.

Instructor Led Classes

Instructor led workouts in a group environment. Our members encourage and push each other to reach their personal goals.

Morning Wake Up

It is 5:30 am, rise and grind! Join us for our Morning Wake Up. No two classes in a week are the same. Be ready for bootcamp circuits, High Intensity Interval Training, as well as the occasional boxing cardio. Class curriculum changes every four weeks.

Hit It All

You will never experience a total body workout better than this! With the integration of TRX equipment, bodyweight exercises, dumbbells, kettlebells, and medicine balls, your heart rate will be high and ready to burn excess fat while increasing lean mass.

Boxing Blitz

Come give our boxing and kickboxing combination a try. You will be sure to burn an immense amount of calories while building up true core strength! Throw in a little bootcamp and you have yourself Boxing Blitz.

Youth Boxing & Fitness Training

Boxing is a sport like no other. It is about environment, and learning about the basics. Our youth boxing teaches children the fundamentals of boxing and fitness, while having FUN. Boxers are taught discipline and develop self-confidence. Ages 9-12 (younger accepted with instructor evaluation and approval).

Instructors & Staff

Andrew

Andrew is the General Manager & Director of Programming. Andrew graduated from McKendree University with a degree in Sports Management and minors in Sport Performance and Sport Psychology. Andrew is also a NCSA Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist. Andrew has extensive experience in personal training of individuals from 12-75 years old and has worked with NCAA Division I & II college athletes. Andrew always brings a positive, enthusiastic, professional attitude to The Loft and loves to guide people in attaining their personal goals.

Anthony

Anthony is a registered USA Boxing Coach and our Head Boxing Coach. Anthony is an evening instructor for adult and youth classes and manages the boxing team. Anthony started boxing at the age of eight and has been coaching boxing for over 20 years. Anthony recently took home the Masters Heavy Weight Championship Belt at the 2016 Ringside World Tournament. Anthony is passionate about boxing and helps people of all ages develop a passion for boxing.

Anthony

Anthony is a registered USA Boxing coach and the Assistant Boxing Coach. Anthony is a morning instructor and can be found taking classes at night. Anthony is also the corner man when our boxers are competing. Anthony also has a passion for boxing and its health benefits. Through boxing Anthony was able to lose more than 40lbs and keep it off.

Leslie

Leslie is a former four sport athlete who took two decades off to raise a family. As an empty-nester, she got back to the gym and developed a passion for sharing what she's learned. She's a qualified instructor in TRX Suspension and Functional Training.

Tessa

Tessa is our evening front desk person and a competitive amateur boxer. Tessa assists with teaching the youth boxing classes and competes on a regular basis. Tessa stands out for her great technique and helps others improve theirs.
