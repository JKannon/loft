About Lights Out Boxing

Anthony started boxing at the age of eight at a small boys club located in the Washington Park area of Illinois. As a kid Anthony didn't take boxing seriously. After a few years of boxing and a move to Missouri Anthony drifted into other sports. It wasn’t until Anthony was out of high school at a weight of more than 300 lbs. that he discovered boxing again at a gym called “The Boxing Gym”. Anthony was able to quickly shave his extra pounds by training for boxing and he started to ponder boxing competitively. The owner of the gym saw something in Anthony and asked him to start coaching his boxing team. That was about ten years ago. Since then Anthony has helped young people train for the Golden Gloves and Ringside tournaments and participate in numerous local amateur events. Anthony finds his success in the many kids and adults he has helped find their own success.

Anthony helps young people find their potential and to realize that they will get out of boxing what they put into it. Anthony wants to inspire people to reach their goals through boxing. But even more than that he wants to bring back an appreciation for the sport that helped him, as a boy, stay off the streets.

Classes

The Lights Out Boxing program meets nightly Monday – Thursday from 7:15 – 8:15 PM and is made up of men and women above the age of 13. A typical week involves cardio, strength & conditioning, Skills & fundamentals training and optional instructor led or one-on-one sparring. Members of the Boxing Team meet for additional instruction Monday – Wednesday from 8:15 – 9:00 PM. We also offer a youth program led by our head coach and our experienced team boxers Tuesday and Thursdays from 6:15 – 7:00 PM.

If you are interested in competitive boxing, want to learn how to defend yourself or just want to get in shape the Lights Out Boxing program is a great choice and is included with our class pass membership.
